About FreshFruit Haven

Founded in 2010, FreshFruit Haven began as a small family farm with a passion for growing the freshest, most flavorful fruits possible.

Today, we partner with dozens of local farms to bring you a wide variety of seasonal fruits, all grown with sustainable practices and picked at peak ripeness.
